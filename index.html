Eevolution Blanket

Both my kids favourite pokemon is Eevee. Growing up I loved pokemon, so I was really excited when my kids got in to it. Every now and then I would ask them what pokemon was their favourite, fully expecting them to say Pikachu. It was not Pikachu, they both said Eevee because it can "grow up" into so many other pokemon. After they both said Eevee for over a year, I started to think about making blankets.
I love crocheting, the repetitive motion helps with my anxiety. I started looking for a pattern, expecting a 3 by 4 foot blanket for each kid. I found patterns for each eevolution, but the blankets would have been about 3 by 3 feet so I decided to add the element symbols. This helped the overall look of the blanket and the borders where no longer against eachother, so off I went to crochet! At the moment I am 3/4 finished both blankets and they will be my kids' Christmas presents.
