Pokemon

Eevee (#133) - normal type.
It is the most adaptable pokemon and can evolve in to many eevolutions to suit its environment.

Vaporeon (#134) - water type.
It lives close to water. It's often mistaken for a mermaid because its long tail is ridged with a fin.

Jolteon (#135) - electric type.
It can use powerful lightning bolts by concentrating the weak electric charges emitted by its cells.

Flareon (#136) - fire type.
It can exhale extremely hot fire by inhaling air that carried to its flame sac, then heated.

Espeon (#196) - psychic type.
It can predict its opponent's next move by quivering the tip of its forked tail.

Umbreon (#197) - dark type.
It can gain a mysterious power by being in moonlight. The rings on it's body will glow when this power is activated

Leafeon (#470) - grass type.
It can produce clean air through photosynthesis by sleeping in the sunlight.

Glaceon (#471) - ice type.
Its body temperature can be controlled at will, creating flurries of diamond dust by freezing the moisture in the atmosphere.

Sylveon (#700) - fairy type.
Its can completely erase its opponent's hostility by using its ribbonlike feelers to send a soothing aura.
