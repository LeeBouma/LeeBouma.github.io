Element Squares

You are not making an element square for Eevee, but I have included Eevee in this list.

  1. Water
    Weakness to electric and grass.
    Strong against fire, ground and rock.

  2. Electric
    Weakness to ground.
    Strong against flying and water.

  3. Fire
    Weakness to water, ground and rock.
    Strong against bug, grass, ice and steel.

  4. Psychic
    Weakness to bug, dark and ghost.
    Strong against fighting and poison.

  5. Normal
    Weakness to fighting.
    Not strong against any elemental type.

  6. Dark
    Weakness to bug, fairy and fighting.
    Strong against ghost and psychic.

  7. Grass
    Weakness to bug, fire, flying, ice and poison.
    Strong against ground, rock and water.

  8. Ice
    Weakness to fighting, fire, rock and steel.
    Strong against dragon, flying, grass and ground.

  9. Fairy
    Weakness to poison and steel.
    Strong against dark, dragon and fighting.
